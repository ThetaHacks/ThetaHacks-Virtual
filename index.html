About

"I think it is possible for ordinary people to choose to be extraordinary."

ThetaHacks is a global high-school virtual hackathon occurring from January 15-18, 2021. Anyone of any skill level is free to submit a project that benefits the world around us. Our event is free, with tons of epic swag, events, and more. During our hackathon, you can form a team of up to 4 people to code anything you want! Attend various workshops and speaker events throughout the long weekend, while hanging out in our (discord) community and getting a ton of free swag. And yes, the more events you attend in our hackathon, the more free swag and merch you get.

FAQ

What is ThetaHacks?▸

ThetaHacks is a 36-hour virtual event where students from all over the world can come together to make something together, whether software or hardware!

Who can participate?▸

Anyone in high school can join! If you've graduated already, consider becoming a mentor! If you are in middle school, email us for details.

Do I need to form a team?▸

You may form a team with up to 4 members! If you would like to go solo, thats perfectly fine too.

Does this cost money?▸

Nope. ThetaHacks is free, and always will be! Everything from swag to workshops is covered for all participants.

What if I can't code?▸

If you can't code, thats totally not a problem! We will be hosting workshops so you can learn, so please join us.

How do I sign up?▸

ThetaHacks has three steps to signing up: (1) sign up on our website, (2) sign up on our devpost, (3) sign up on our events page.

Where do I get more information?▸

Check out our Devpost and our info page for more info!

I have another question!▸

Great, we're always open to questions! Feel free to contact us at hello@thetahacks.tech! Or, check out more info about ThetaHacks on our info page!
